A little about Gotham

Gotham City is one of the oldest cities in the east coast.
It was founded by dutch settlers in 1609. Originally named
New Rotterdam, Gotham City as we now know it came to be
With the help of four prominent wealthy families, the the Waynes,
the Kanes, the Elliots and the Cobblepots.

The legend of the Batman

Gotham City has a well known reputation of being home to "superheroes".
Most famous among them being the Batman. While some may debate his
existence he is definitely real. Whether he is human or of the supernatural it is clear that the Batman is here to help stop crime.
